## Notes

- Images are stored as WebP files to keep payloads small.
- Hero assets are under 300 KB, card assets are under 150 KB, and thumbnails are under 100 KB at the time of download.
- Attribution is documented for transparency; Pexels marks these images as free to use on their source pages.
- Images were visually checked to avoid AI-looking, fake certificate, fake government, foreign-currency, unrelated-uniform, and overly casual imagery.
