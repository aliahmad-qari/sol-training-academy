2. GENERAL REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Refunds are available in the following circumstances:

✅ REFUND ELIGIBLE:
- Service was NOT delivered as promised (major failure or non-delivery)
- Deliverables do NOT meet the agreed specifications after reasonable revision attempts
- Payment was charged in error or duplicated
- Service was cancelled by us without cause

❌ NON-REFUNDABLE (Digital Goods Exception):
Under Australian Consumer Law, digital goods (once accessed) are generally non-refundable due to their nature. This includes:
- NDIS training materials and curriculum (once download access granted)
- Custom NDIS registration documents and policy packs (once generated)
- Software systems and automation tools (once deployed or API keys issued)
- Website hosting activation and custom website builds (once site goes live)
- Bookkeeping software setup and data entry (once work commences)
- Consultation and advisory services (once delivered)
- Email templates, contract templates, and policy documents (once provided)

❌ "CHANGE OF MIND" REFUNDS:
Australian Consumer Law provides an exception for digital goods. We do NOT offer refunds simply because you changed your mind, but we may offer alternatives (see Section 3).

3. ALTERNATIVES TO REFUNDS (CHANGE OF MIND)

If you've changed your mind about a service, we offer these alternatives instead of refund:

a) Service Credit
- Credit applied toward a future service of equal or greater value
- Valid for 12 months from issue date
- Can be used by you or transferred to a team member

b) Modified Deliverables
- If the deliverable didn't meet your expectations, we'll modify it at no additional cost
- Up to 2 revision rounds included with most packages
- Additional revisions billed at $150/hour

c) Deferred Delivery
- Postpone service to a later date (within 12 months)
- No expiration on deferred services
- Can transfer to a team member if organization changes

d) Partial Service Refund (Discretionary)
- For bundled services, we may refund the portion not yet commenced
- Requires request within 14 days of purchase
- Subject to management discretion and contract terms

7. SERVICE FAILURES & GUARANTEED REMEDIES

If we fail to deliver as promised, you are entitled to remedy:

A) DELIVERY DELAYS
- Delay of 5+ working days: Service credit equal to 5% of service cost
- Delay of 10+ working days: Service credit equal to 10% of service cost
- Delay of 20+ working days: Full refund or 25% service credit (your choice)

B) QUALITY FAILURES
- Deliverables don't meet agreed specifications: Up to 2 free revisions
- After 2 revisions, no improvement: 25% refund or service credit

C) PAYMENT FAILURES
- Duplicate charge: Full refund of duplicate amount within 48 hours
- Unauthorized charge: Immediate refund + dispute support

8. AUSTRALIAN CONSUMER LAW COMPLIANCE

Your Consumer Guarantees:

Under Australian Consumer Law, you have guarantees that:
1. Services will be provided with due care and skill
2. Goods (websites, documents) are of acceptable quality
3. Goods match the description provided to you
4. Services are completed within a reasonable time

Your Rights if Services Fail:
- Right to remedy (repair, replace, refund)
- Right to compensation for loss or damage
- Right to choose remedy (refund preferred if service fails entirely)
- Right to complain to consumer affairs body

Digital Goods Exception:
- Once digital goods are delivered/accessed, they cannot be returned for refund
- However, if goods are faulty or unfit for purpose, you retain remedies
