5. DATA SECURITY & ENCRYPTION

We implement the following security measures:

- End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ protocol
- Secure Payment Processing: Payment data is processed securely through Stripe's PCI-DSS compliant platform; we do not store full credit card details
- Role-Based Access Control: Only authorized staff can access your information, with access limited to service delivery
- Secure File Storage: Uploaded files (logos, documents, briefs) are stored on encrypted servers with restricted access
- Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and penetration testing
- Backup & Disaster Recovery: Your data is backed up securely and can be recovered in case of system failure
- Password Protection: Administrative accounts are protected by strong passwords and multi-factor authentication
- Compliance Logging: All access to sensitive data is logged and monitored for suspicious activity
